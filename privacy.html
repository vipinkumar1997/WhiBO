Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how WhiBO ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our anonymous chat service.

1. Information We Collect

WhiBO is designed to provide an anonymous chatting experience. We collect and store minimal information:

  • Technical Information: IP addresses (temporarily), device information, browser type, and operating system for service operation and security purposes.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous statistics about how users interact with our service to improve user experience.
  • Chat Content: Messages exchanged during chat sessions are temporarily processed on our servers for transmission but are not permanently stored.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our service
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical and security issues
  • To monitor and analyze usage patterns and trends
  • To improve our service and user experience

3. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Cookies and Tracking

WhiBO uses essential cookies to enable core functionality such as maintaining user sessions and preferences. We may also use analytics cookies to understand how visitors interact with our service. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

5. Third-Party Services

We may employ third-party companies and individuals to facilitate our service, provide service-related assistance, or analyze how our service is used. These third parties may have access to your information only to perform tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

6. Legal Disclosure

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court or government agency).

7. Children's Privacy

WhiBO is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child without parental consent, we will take steps to remove that information from our servers.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to — and maintained on — computers located outside of your state, province, country, or other governmental jurisdiction where the data protection laws may differ. By using our service, you consent to this transfer.

9. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • The right to access information we hold about you
  • The right to request correction or deletion of your information
  • The right to restrict or object to processing of your information
  • The right to data portability

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@whibo.com.
